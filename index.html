This app notifies ⏰ as soon as vaccine slot is marked available for next day in your target area 📌 (by PIN or by District) for selected age group/s.

Since slots are getting booked very quickly (in few minutes), this app will ensure that you are notified immediately about newly available slot.
Simply open this HTML page in your 💻 PC browser and focus on your other work.

🔊 Keep volume level of device audible enough so that notification alarm ring can be heard.

Slot avaialbilitiy is checked every 5 seconds. An alarm will ring as soon as the new slot is available for next day.

Simply refresh the page to start a new search. Search is over as soon as available slot is notified.
Keep yourself logged-in in Setu app and/or COWIN portal. As soon as alarm rings, you should be quickly booking an appointment.
